Monte Carlo Simulations

  • Category: Research Project
  • Topics: Monte Carlo Simulations, Geometric Brownian Motion, Portfolio Building
  • Project date: November, 2018

Use Case

This project explores the applications of Monte Carlo Simulations to analyze stock price trajectories and allocate capital to build high performing investment portfolios.

Implementation

In this project, I modelled stock price as a Geometric Brownian Motion process and ran simulations to observe price movements of 4 different stocks listed on the National Stock Exchange (NSE). For each stock, a million simulations were run to analyze performance over a 10 year period. On the basis of performance observed, weights were assigned to each stock in order to build a portfolio. This portfolio generated a return of 14.98% annually from November 2018 to June 2023. Interestingly, the entire market made only 12.64% in the same period.
